ANSI codes for coloring strings in terminals:

\e[NNmText to be colored\e[0m
------                  ------
Start color             Back to normal

NN is a color code. Some colors are as follows:

Color       NN(fg)  NN(bg)
--------------------------
black:      30      40
red:        31      41
green:      32      42
brown:      33      43
blue:       34      44
magenta:    35      45
cyan:       36      46
gray:       37      47
